LAMB BEBRA


This is not to say that lamb on the table is familiar phenomenon. It is not cooked and eaten as often as meat of other varieties, although lamb, especially young lamb, is a dietary easily digestible product, there is no cholesterol in it. Regular consumption of lamb speeds up the metabolism, therefore it should be included in the diet for those who want to lose extra kilograms without impoverishing your food.

Cooking time: 180 minutes
Price: 35 manat
Ingredients:
  • Condiments (thyme, oregano, white pepper): 1-2 teaspoons
  • Tabasco sauce: 5 tablespoons
  • Dry wine: half a tablespoon
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Honey: half a tablespoon
  • Sugar: 3 tablespoons
  • Paprika: 3 teaspoons
  • Mustard: to taste
  • Garlic: 2 wedges
  • Lamb ribs: 1kg
  • Butter: 100 g
  • Lemon: 1 pc.
