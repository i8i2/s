CHICKEN ON PADS


It is not difficult to cook chicken on skewers, even for a young housewife. But please any gourmet. So, we are preparing the right products. I advise you to try the meat with mayonnaise with an extraordinary taste of the dish.

Cooking time: 25 minutes plus marinating meat.
Price: 40 manat.
Ingredients:
  • Mayonnaise of any brand: 100 grams.
  • Any chicken seasoning: ½ teaspoon.
  • Sesame seeds: 2 tablespoons.
  • Vegetable oil: 1 tablespoon.
  • Chicken pulp: 400 grams.
  • One bow head.
  • Salt, optional.
